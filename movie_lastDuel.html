The Last Duel

The era of absurd power and barbarism, France in the 14th century. Margrit, the wife of the historic Karuju family, was away while her husband Jang was away. He is humiliated by "Zach," a friend of "Jang" who suddenly came in. Jacques, who committed something unforgivable, forces Margrit to remain silent, but... "Margrit" is a song that you have to endure as soon as you open your mouth. Be prepared for dishonor and take courage to accuse "Jack" of the crime. "Zach," who has power on his back, strongly insists on his innocence, The "Jang" to protect the family and their honor is... The winning person soon requests a duel trial in which justice is judged. If "Jang" loses the duel, "Margrit" is destined to be executed immediately.

A single duel determines the fate of the three!
