The Boy Behind the Door

Two boys who were returning home from class were kidnapped. Kevin and Bobby are trapped in an unfamiliar house with all the doors locked outside in a deserted forest. To make matters worse, Kevin is left alone in a secret place in the house with his hands and feet bound.

Who was the culprit who kidnapped the two boys and why?
And will the boys be able to escape safely?
