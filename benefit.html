Point accumulation
  • Basic points accumulation
  • · Set aside 5-10% of the paid amount for movie tickets
  • - General member: 5% accumulation
    - VIP member: 10% accumulation (However, only 5% of VIP selection criteria are reflected)
  • · 2% of the paid payment amount at the store
  • · After the payment is completed, the points will be set aside as points to be used on the next day of the screening for the movie and the next day of purchase for the store.
  • · Points will be accumulated only when a member logs in and uses online services, presents membership cards at the on-site ticket office or kiosk, or provides information to confirm that he or she is a member.
  • · Except for the amount discounted through Megabox, affiliate discount, and point payment, the actual amount will be set aside based on the amount paid by the customer through cash or credit card.
  • · Some movies, goods, theaters, and payment methods may not be accumulated or a separate reserve rate may be applied, and details will be notified separately to the relevant products, theaters, etc.
  • · This is the standard point for VIP selection.
  • · You can earn points only before the start of the movie, which is exposed to the ticket, and you cannot accumulate points after the movie is screened and the store is purchased.
  • Theater excluding point accumulation
  • · In some theaters, you can't earn points when you buy a store.
  • - Excluding store accumulation: Gyeongsan Hayang, Gyeongju, Gongju, Gimcheon, Namyangju, Namwon, Namchuncheon, Bukdaegu (Chilgok), Sokcho, Suncheon, Art Nine, Ansan Jungang, Wonju, Incheon Nonhyeon, Jecheon, Jincheon, Advanced, Cheongju Sanchang, Chungju, Chungju, Paju Geumchon
  • · You can't earn points when watching The boutique Private.
  • Other Earnings Guidance
  • · Points will not be earned if you receive a price discount through group movie viewing and store purchases.
  • · Tickets issued in Megabox will earn points based on the price of tickets sold regardless of the price of the movie.
  • · Tickets paid with mobile coupons (gifticons, shows, etc.) will not be eligible for points.
  • · Points will be earned if the membership information is the same when booking tickets through the consignee (Interpark, YES24 etc.).

Effective Period and Disappearance
  • Point validity period
  • · Basic points: 24 months after the date of accumulation (when paid purchase of tickets and stores)
  • · Gift points: The date at the end of the month, three months after the date of the gift.
  • · Event accumulation point: Different expiration dates for each event (requires individual verification)
  • Point extinction
  • · Points that have expired will automatically expire after the end of each month.
  • · The decimation criterion is the point with the shortest validity of the remaining points, and the decimated points are not recovered.
  • · If the scheduled expiration date of the retention point is reached, the details related to the point extinction will be notified by text or e-mail at least 15 days in advance. Also, you can check the expected points when logging in to the homepage and mobile web/app.
A birthday coupon
  • I'll give you a birthday coupon!
  • · Birthday coupons are only available to Megabox members.
  • · Birthday coupons are given once a year, and if they are issued once, they are not issued additionally.
  • · Birthday coupons are issued based on birthdays registered in member information.
  • - Coupon will be issued two weeks before the birthday, and the validity period will be four weeks from the date of issue (e.g. March 2nd for birthdays on March 16th, and can be used until March 30th).
  • · You can check the issued coupon at 'My Megabox > Coupon' after logging in.
  • · Based on the date of coupon issuance, it is only available to normal members. (Excluding customers with dormant member status, etc.)
  • · The contents of the coupon, payment schedule, and how to use it are subject to internal circumstances.
