Threads: Our Tapestry of Love

"I'll protect you" My first love "Ren" and "Aoi" when I was 12. We recognize each other's wounds at a glance and embrace each other. The relationship between the two begins. We had a great time together for a while The Aoi family leaves as if they were being chased and breaks up. "I think there's a thread of fate". Ren, who kept the wish bracelet from "Aoi" for 8 years. One day, the wish bracelet is broken, and the two reunite like fate. After that, coincidental encounters and breakups are repeated, Whenever that happens, there are already different people next to each other, so we cross each other.
