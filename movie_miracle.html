Miracle

There's only a railroad that I can go back and forth, It's a village where there's no train station. As of today, Joonkyung (Park Jungmin), who sent the 54th letter to Cheong Wa Dae, has only one goal! That is, a train station will be built in the village. Despite the opposition of Taeyoon (Lee Seongmin), the father of the principle engineer, that the train station is not possible, Joonkyung, who insisted on staying in the village with his sister Bokyeong (Lee Sookyung) and going back and forth between school and school for five hours. With his self-proclaimed muse Rahee (Lim Yoonah), who recognized his extraordinaryness in his wackiness at once, Spelling class for convincing letter writing,Scholarship quiz test to gain popularity, Until the president's math contest!
Joonkyung's own efforts to build a train station continue.

There is no giving up.
Until the train stops!
