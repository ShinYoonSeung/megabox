The Call

What year is it now?
"Seoyeon" (Park Shinhye) who came back home after a long time. After connecting an old phone at home, he calls a strange woman named Young-sook (Jeon Jong-seo). Seoyeon realizes that Young-sook was a person who lived in the same house 20 years ago, and from then on, the two build friendships.
"I told you, don't hang up."
Then one day, "Seo-yeon" and "Young-sook" make dangerous choices to change each other's lives in their respective present. Young-sook saved Seo-yeon's father, who died 20 years ago, and Seo-yeon told her the future of Young-sook. However, "Young-sook," who learned of her terrible future, begins to threaten "Seo-yeon" with an unexpected explosion.!

A phone call that broke the taboo.
The murderer opens his eyes.
