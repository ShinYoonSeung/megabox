Never Gonna Snow Again

"One, two, three. Every time I exhale, I feel lighter. Like dust!" "Zenia," a hypnotist who looks into people's hearts' sorrows and cravings through her special abilities. As everyone is eager to meet him, the hypnosis of "Zenia," hidden in the mystery, begins to captivate people.
