Otac, Father

A small rural village in Serbia. Nicola, the father of two children, who has been dismissed and is barely living a day as a daily worker. The wife, who could not endure poverty and hunger, acted extremely in anger at the company, Corrupt social welfare departments deprive parents of custody of two children only for their own benefit. Nicola, who eventually lost her children despite her hard efforts to protect her family, In a situation where no one can trust, as a one-dimensional effort to protect his family, He decides on a long journey to the capital, Belgrade, which is over 300 kilometers away.

Despite all the impossibilities, For the right and justice to restore their children, My father, Nicola, never stops walking.
