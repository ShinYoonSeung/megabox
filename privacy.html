Article 2. (Duration of Processing and Retention of Personal Information)
The company processes and holds personal information within the period of possession and use of personal information in accordance with laws and regulations or the period of use of personal information agreed upon when collecting personal information from the information subject.
Each personal information processing and retention period is as follows.
1. Essential collection items for service use: Retention until withdrawal.
If it falls under the following reasons, hold it until the end of the relevant reason.

1) Non-member collection items.
①Hold it for 7 days after viewing or cancellation.
②Inquiries about customer centers, inquiries about partnerships/advertisements/subsidiary businesses, and holds them up to 6 months after processing is completed when using the online information center.
2) To minimize damage caused by indiscriminate withdrawal and re-registration, and to handle complaints caused by change of mind after customer withdrawal, hold minimum customer information for 30 days after withdrawal.
3) In the case where an investigation or investigation is in progress due to a violation of relevant laws and regulations, until the end of the investigation or investigation,
4) When the bond/debt relationship remains due to the use of the website, until the relevant bond/debt relationship is settled.

2. Selective collection items: Retention and destruction until the specified period when obtaining consent for collection.
3. Dormant Member: Retard after holding for one year from the time of storage as a dormant customer.

1) Dormant members: members who have not used the service for more than one year.
2) Conditions for dormant membership conversion.
- Customers who have not logged in to the website, mobile web, or app for more than a year.
- A customer who has no history of point trading for more than a year.
- Customers who have not issued or deleted membership cards for more than a year.
3) Dormant member in advance notice.
- Announcement date: 30 days before the change of sleep.
- Information items: facts separated separately, scheduled dormant date, and personal information items stored separately.
- Information method: Information in one way, such as e-mail, written, and copy transmission.

4. Providing goods or services: Until goods, service supply and payment of charges, and settlement are completed.
However, in the case of the following reasons, until the end of the relevant period,

1) Records of transactions, such as indication, advertisement, contract details, and performance, pursuant to the Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, etc. Act.
- Records of the user's identity verification posted: 6 months.
- Records of withdrawal of contract or subscription, payment, supply of goods, etc.: 5 years
- Records of consumer complaints or disputes: 3 years
2) Keeping data confirming communication facts pursuant to Article 41 of the Communications Secret Protection Act.
- Records of web page visits and log-in: 3 months

Article 5. (The rights, obligations, and exercise method of the data subject)
1. The data subject (including the legal representative for children under the age of 14) may exercise the following personal information protection rights against the company at any time.

1) Request to read personal information.
2) If there's an error, ask for correction.
3) Request deletion.
4) Request to stop processing.

2. If you want to view and correct online subscription information, the data subject (including legal representative for children under the age of 14) can directly read and correct it by clicking "Member withdrawal" and "Modify Personal Information" on the website, or contact the Dream Center (1544-0070).
3. The exercise of rights under paragraph 1 can be made through written, telephone, e-mail, or mock-up remittance (FAX) to the company, and the company will take action without delay.
4. If the data subject (including the legal representative for children under the age of 14) requests correction or deletion of personal information errors, the company does not use or provide the personal information until correction or deletion is completed.
5. The exercise of rights under paragraph 1 may be made through an agent such as a legal representative of the data subject or a person who has been delegated. In this case, you must submit a power of attorney in accordance with attached Form 11 of the Enforcement Rule of the Personal Information Protection Act.
6. The data subject shall not infringe on the personal information and privacy of the data subject or other person handled by the company in violation of relevant laws such as the Personal Information Protection Act.
Article 6. (Procedures and Methods for Destruction of Personal Information)
1. If personal information becomes unnecessary, such as the lapse of the period of personal information retention or the achievement of the purpose of processing, the company will destroy the personal information without delay.
2. If personal information must be preserved in accordance with other laws and regulations even after the period of personal information retention agreed by the data subject has elapsed or the purpose of processing has been achieved, the personal information will be transferred to a separate database (DB).
3. The procedure and method of destroying personal information are as follows.

1) The process of destroying it.
①The company destroys personal information without delay when personal information becomes unnecessary, such as the lapse of the period of personal information retention or the achievement of the purpose of processing.
②In the event of a reason for requesting destruction, the company destroys personal information without delay and notifies the relevant result.
2) How to do it.
①Electronic file type: Destroy using a method such as a low level format so that the record cannot be reproduced.
②Paper document: Crushing or incinerating with a shredder.
