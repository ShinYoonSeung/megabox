On the Line

Just one phone call! As soon as I got caught, I got caught! A phone call from Busan construction site staff. From daughter's hospital bills to apartment intermediate payments due to voice phishing calls, On the same day, numerous people lose money like their lives. Former detective Seo Jun (Byun Yo-han), the head of the field work team, wanted to recover 3 billion won in money from his family and colleagues. It starts tracking the voice phishing organization. Seojun finally infiltrated the call center located in China, Securing personal information, receiving scripts from the planning office, recruiting withdrawal books, working at currency exchanges, and even a large call center! I was surprised by the systematically organized scale of voice phishing. There, he finally faces Kwak Moo-yeol (Kim Moo-yeol), the main character of the voice digging into the hopes and fears of the victims. And I found out that he was planning a new all-out war worth 30 billion won.

The substance of voice phishing that is organized more than you can imagine!
Follow him to the end and get him back!
