Pokemon the Movie: Secrets of the Jungl

From the Pokemon's Paradise Pride Forest, far from the human village, "Zarudo," which lived with the crowd following strict laws, You accidentally find a human child by the river. I couldn't turn a blind eye to "Zarudo" Breaking the law of the forest, he gave the child the name "Coco". They decide to live alone apart from the group. 10 years later, "Coco," a boy who believes that he is Pokemon, I met Jiwoo and Pikachu who came to the Pride Forest to learn about the new world.

On the other hand, he's aiming for a healing spring that protects the pride forest. Dangerous humans' footsteps begin to come in!
Can Coco, the child of the jungle, work together with friends to protect the forest?
