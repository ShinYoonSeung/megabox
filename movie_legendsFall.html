Legends Of The Fall

1913, Montana, USA. Colonel Ludlow, who settled in a peaceful place to avoid war, Three sons Alfred, Tristan, and Samuel. Samuel, the youngest who went abroad to study, brought his fiance Susanna home. Laughter begins to dwell on the quiet ranch. But for a while, All three sons participated in World War I. A sad fate that cannot be countered comes to them.

Life and death, love and pain, the beauty that exists in war and peace, An epic that resonates with everyone's soul resonates again.
