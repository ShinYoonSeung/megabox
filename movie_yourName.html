Your name

I've never met you before. I'm looking for you. The upcoming comet miracle begins in a thousand years. Taki, a boy living in Tokyo, and Mitsuha, a girl living in the countryside, have a strange dream of changing their bodies. Strange family, unfamiliar friends, unfamiliar scenery...In repeated dreams and flowing time, I finally realize it. Did we switch? The two who will never meet. It becomes fate that must be met. Taki and Mitsuha, who are becoming increasingly friends by checking the notes left to each other, realized that they were connected in a special way when their bodies no longer changed, and they are going to meet Mitsuha...

Someone I don't want to forget. Someone you can't forget. What's your name?
